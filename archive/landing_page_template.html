NFL DFS Boom/Bust Analysis

Interactive Visualization Tool for DraftKings Data

Analyze NFL DraftKings player data with an interactive scatter plot visualization. Identify optimal leverage opportunities, evaluate boom/bust rates, and make data-driven decisions for your daily fantasy lineups.

Key Features

  • Interactive Scatter Plot: Visualize boom%, leverage, and ownership with customizable axes
  • Player Headshots: See actual player photos and team logos for easy identification
  • Advanced Filtering: Filter by position, team, salary range, and ownership percentage
  • Data Table View: Sortable table with per-column filters and global search
  • Quadrant Analysis: Color-coded regions to identify best plays and avoid traps
  • Zoom & Explore: Drag to zoom in on specific data clusters
  • Mobile Responsive: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
Launch Visualization

How to Use

Chart View: Click on team logos or position buttons to filter. Adjust the sliders for salary and ownership ranges. Change axes using the dropdowns to explore different correlations. Drag on the chart to zoom in.

Data Table: Sort by any column. Use the filter icons to narrow down specific positions, teams, or value ranges. Use the search bar for quick player lookup.

Understanding the Quadrants

Green (Best): High boom%, high leverage - optimal tournament plays
Amber (Contrarian): Low ownership, high ceiling - leverage opportunities
Grey (Popular): High ownership, lower leverage - proceed with caution
Red (Avoid): Low boom%, low leverage - generally avoid in tournaments
